Adriana van de Guchte

I am a research microbiologist working at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai in the Bakel Lab. My primary research interests are pathogen surveillance and the microbiome. Within pathogen surveillance I have been tracking the SARS-nCoV-19 pandemic as well as MRSA bacteriema within the NYC area.

One of my specialities is biofilm research, a complex process due to the nature of biofilm growth. However I am experienced with a working and reliable assay and am currently using it for a largescale project. I am also becoming proficient with various coding languages, working with Python, SQL, and HTML.
